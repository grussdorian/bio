Behind the Lens

Why?

I'm fascinated by the human condition. I go around and talk to people. Much of it is because there's this innate desire to socialize and connect with others (insert distributed systems joke here :P) Heavily inspired by Saul Leiter, Robert Frank and Helen Levitt. I shoot mainly in 35 and 75 on a sony A6700
